Manga Search App

Find something new to read.

Use the search bar below to find your favorite Manga! Under each Manga cover you will find a link that leads to that Manga's MyAnimeList page to read more. Some search terms are limited by the API which may yield to inconsistent results in the English and Japanese names. This was addressed in an update to the API in November 2022 before being rolled back due to app instability with no further comment from the devs. If your first search doesn't match, please tweak your query and try again!

The list will automatically reset whenever you search for a new term or you can opt to use the reset button below to clear it manually.
