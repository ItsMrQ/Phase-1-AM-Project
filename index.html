Manga Search App

Find something new to read.

Use the search bar below to find your favorite Manga! Under each Manga cover you will find a link that leads to that Manga's MyAnimeList page to read more. Some search terms are limited by the API which may yield to inconsistent results in the English and Japanese names.

The list will automatically reset whenever you search for a new term or you can opt to the use the reset button below to clear it manually.
